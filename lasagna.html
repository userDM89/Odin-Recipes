Lasagna

We will be showing you the steps to create the perfect stuffed lasagna. Incredients and steps are listed below.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beaf
  • 3/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon Salt

Guide

  1. Make homemade meat sauce. This is done by sauteing an onion in olive oil until it begins to soften.
  2. Make parmesean ricotta cheese mixture. This is what gives the lasagna the creamy filling.
  3. Use your favorite lasagna noodles. There are many noodle options so the choice is yours.
  4. Bring on the cheese! The best lasanga is full of cheese so use liberal amounts.
  5. Bake covered for 15 minutes. Bake until the cheese is bubbling and then let cool for 10 minutes.
