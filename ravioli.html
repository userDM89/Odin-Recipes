Four Cheese Ravioli

We will be giving you the steps and ingredients to create the perfect four cheese ravioli dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Semolina Pasta Flour
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose Flour
  • 4 whole eggs
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons water

Guide

  1. Combine all pasta ingredients and mix together to make a stiff dough.
  2. Create raviolis out of the dough.
  3. Place cheese inside of ravioli and cover so they are sealed.
  4. Boil raviolis until fully cooked and serve fresh.
